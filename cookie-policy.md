<p class="text-[14px] text-[#666] pb-[10px] leading-[24px] font-[500]">
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website.
They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to
website owners. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies
remain on your device after you close your browser, while session cookies are deleted
when you close your browser.
</p>
